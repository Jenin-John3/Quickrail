The Problem

Booking a train is harder than it should be.

Over 14 million passengers travel on Tamil Nadu's rail network every day. A large number of them still rely on agents to book tickets — paying extra because the official system is too complicated to use on a phone.

Today's experience

IRCTC requires account registration, Aadhaar verification, and captchas before you can even see fares
Pages time out on slow 2G/3G connections common in rural areas
Booking agents charge ₹50–150 extra per ticket as a convenience fee
Elderly and first-time passengers frequently miss trains due to booking confusion
